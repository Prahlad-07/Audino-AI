## Overview

**Audino AI** is an AI-powered platform that transforms **audio and video content into meaningful, interactive experiences**.

With Audino, users can:
- Upload or paste a YouTube/public media link
- Generate summaries and chapters
- Chat with meeting content
- Extract insights from calls and recordings
- Detect harmful or unsafe speech patterns

It is designed to save time, improve productivity, and make content easier to consume and act on.

---

## Features

### 1. Content AI
Convert YouTube videos, public audio, or video URLs into:
- Interactive discussions
- Smart summaries
- Chapters
- Chat-based exploration

### 2. Meetings AI
Turn meetings into:
- Structured summaries
- Key takeaways
- Actionable insights
- Conversational Q&A

### 3. Call Recording AI
Analyze recorded calls and generate:
- Smart summaries
- Important highlights
- Useful notes for follow-up

### 4. Hate Speech AI
Detect unsafe or harmful speech patterns to support safer and more responsible communication.

### 5. Clean and Modern UI
- Mobile-first design
- Simple navigation
- Smooth user experience
- Attractive promotional and onboarding screens
